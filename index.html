About

I am a PhD candidate in particle physics at the University of Manitoba, working on the MOLLER experiment. My research focuses on magnetic spectrometer design, radiation shielding studies, and precision characterization of photomultiplier tubes (PMTs), including quantum efficiency and gain measurements. I previously completed an MSc in theoretical astrophysics and a BSc in astrophysics.

Research Interests

  • Precision electroweak physics and the MOLLER experiment
  • Detector development: PMTs, light guides, optical calibration, radiation studies
  • Nuclear & particle instrumentation, data analysis, and simulation
  • Cosmology and dark energy models

دربارهٔ من
